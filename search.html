What's a cost center?

Think of a cost center as an internal university funding account. Each cost center is identified with a unique number and is controlled by a department. A cost center may have children cost centers for more detailed accounting.

A "revenue" cost center refers to a cost center that is solely used to account for money collected through sales or other means (vending machine sales, for example).
